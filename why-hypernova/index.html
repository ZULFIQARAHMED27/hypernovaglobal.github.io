Why HyperNova?

Most automation projects fail because agencies focus on tools instead of your actual business problem. I built HyperNova to do it differently.

The honest version

I'm Zulfiqar — a solo AI and automation consultant based in India, working with SMBs and startups worldwide.

I started HyperNova because I kept seeing the same problem: small businesses drowning in manual work, paying agencies large retainers for systems that were overbuilt, fragile, and handed off with zero documentation. The founders were left with something they couldn't maintain or understand.

My approach is different. I work directly with you — no middlemen, no junior developers, no account managers. You get a system that's built right, documented clearly, and sized for where your business actually is today.

Every project gets my full attention. I only take on work I can deliver well.

How I work differently

You talk to the builder

There's no account manager between you and the person doing the work. You get direct access to me — which means faster decisions, clearer communication, and no lost context.

Outcome-first, always

I start with your business problem, not a tool I want to use. If automation won't genuinely help you, I'll tell you that upfront — even if it means a smaller project.

Built to last, not impress

No overengineered systems. No fragile workflows held together by duct tape. I build simple, reliable automations you can actually maintain — and I document everything.

Sized for your stage

I don't sell enterprise solutions to 5-person teams. Every system is scoped for where you are now, with room to grow — so you're not paying for complexity you don't need.

Fast delivery

No long agency timelines. Most projects are scoped, built, and handed over in 1–5 weeks. You see progress quickly and can start getting value fast.

Global reach, personal service

Based in India, working async with clients across the US, UK, Europe, and beyond. Timezone flexibility means I can fit around your schedule, not the other way around.
